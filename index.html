How to Play

The Game

Dealt 5 cards — click cards to HOLD, then DRAW to replace the rest. Make a paying hand to win.

Multi-Hand (OPTIONS → Hands)

Play 1–4 hands at once. Your holds copy into every hand; each hand then draws its own cards from its own deck. Total bet = bet × hands, and every hand pays separately.

Games (OPTIONS → Game)

Jacks or Better · Bonus & Double Bonus · Double Double Bonus (kicker bonuses) · Deuces Wild (every 2 is wild — min paying hand is three of a kind).

Double Up

After a win, DOUBLE UP to gamble the total: beat the dealer's card to double. Tie pushes; lower loses it all.

Keys

1–5 hold · Enter/Space deal/draw/take · D double · M mute · Esc close
