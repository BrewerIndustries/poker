How to Play

The Game

You're dealt 5 cards. Click cards to HOLD the ones you want to keep, then press DRAW to replace the rest. Make a paying hand (a pair of Jacks or better) to win.

Double Up

After any win, DOUBLE UP to gamble: pick a card higher than the dealer's to double your winnings. Tie pushes; lower loses it all. Repeat as far as your nerve holds — or TAKE WIN.

Bets & Denomination

BET UP cycles 1→5 coins; BET MAX wagers 5 and deals. The denomination badge sets each credit's cash value (shown under CREDITS). The max-bet Royal Flush pays the 4000 jackpot.
